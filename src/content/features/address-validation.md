## Address Validation

Catch address errors before the order ships. Real-time validation checks addresses at checkout and suggests corrections — reducing failed deliveries and saving you money.

### How It Works

When a customer enters their shipping address, Checkout Ninja validates it in real-time against address databases. If issues are found, the customer sees a suggestion to correct their address before completing the order.

### Key Features

- **Real-time validation** — Addresses are checked as the customer types, not after submission
- **Smart suggestions** — Offer corrected addresses when issues are detected (typos, missing apartment numbers, etc.)
- **International support** — Validate addresses across all countries you ship to
- **Configurable strictness** — Choose to warn customers or block checkout for invalid addresses
- **Reduce failed deliveries** — Prevent shipping to addresses that don't exist or can't receive packages
