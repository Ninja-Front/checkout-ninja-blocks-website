## Trust & Security Blocks

Build customer confidence at the most critical moment — payment. These 7 blocks let you display trust signals exactly where they matter most.

### Available Blocks

- **Trust Badge** — Display security badges and certifications like Norton, McAfee, or custom trust seals directly in your checkout
- **Payment Icons** — Show accepted payment methods (Visa, Mastercard, Apple Pay, etc.) so customers know their preferred option is available
- **Review Widget** — Pull in your Trustpilot, Judge.me, or Loox rating to show social proof at checkout
- **Guarantee Badge** — Display money-back guarantee, satisfaction guarantee, or return policy badges to reduce purchase anxiety
- **Security Seal** — Add SSL indicators and secure checkout messaging to reassure customers their data is protected
- **Custom Image** — Upload any trust-building image, certification logo, or brand badge
- **Social Proof Counter** — Show real-time data like "12 people bought this today" or "347 happy customers" to create urgency and trust
