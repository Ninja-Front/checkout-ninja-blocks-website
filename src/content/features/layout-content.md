## Layout & Content Blocks

Make your checkout feel like an extension of your brand. These 6 blocks give you full control over the visual structure and content of your checkout page.

### Available Blocks

- **Heading** — Add section titles, instructions, or callouts anywhere in the checkout flow
- **Image** — Display banners, promotional graphics, or brand imagery to reinforce your identity
- **Divider** — Create visual separation between checkout sections for a cleaner layout
- **Progress Bar** — Show customers how close they are to free shipping or a bonus reward
- **Accordion** — Organize content into collapsible sections to keep checkout clean and scannable
- **Banner** — Display important messages, promotions, or announcements with customizable styling
