## Conversion Booster Blocks

Turn your checkout page into a revenue driver. These 3 blocks help you increase average order value and reduce cart abandonment at the most critical moment.

### Available Blocks

- **Upsell** — Show recommended products, complementary items, or bundle offers right in the checkout. Customers can add with one click without leaving the page
- **Coupon List** — Display available discount codes and promotions so customers don't leave checkout to hunt for coupons. Reduces abandonment and increases satisfaction
- **Shipping Protection** — Offer shipping insurance or package protection as an opt-in add-on. Generate additional revenue while giving customers peace of mind
