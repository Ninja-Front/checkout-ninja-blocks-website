## Custom Field Blocks

Capture the information your business needs — right at checkout. These 8 blocks let you add any type of input field without writing code.

### Available Blocks

- **Text Input** — Single-line text field for names, order references, or any short text
- **Text Area** — Multi-line field perfect for gift messages, special instructions, or delivery notes
- **Dropdown** — Let customers choose from a predefined list of options
- **Date Picker** — Capture delivery dates, appointment times, or any date-based input
- **Checkbox** — Add opt-ins, agreements, or boolean choices (e.g., "This is a gift")
- **Radio Buttons** — Present mutually exclusive options in a clear, visual format
- **Number Input** — Capture numeric values with min/max validation
- **File Upload** — Let customers attach files like custom artwork, prescriptions, or documents
