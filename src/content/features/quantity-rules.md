## Quantity Rules

Enforce product quantity limits automatically at checkout. Set rules based on country, customer type, product, or any condition — no manual work needed.

### How It Works

Define quantity limits with conditions. When a customer's cart exceeds the limit, they see a clear message explaining the restriction. The checkout button is disabled until they adjust their order.

### Common Use Cases

- **Per-customer limits** — Limit purchases to prevent reselling or hoarding
- **Country-based limits** — Set different quantity caps for different markets
- **Product-level limits** — Restrict specific items (limited editions, samples, etc.)
- **Time-based limits** — Enforce stricter limits during sales or launches
- **B2B vs D2C limits** — Set higher limits for wholesale customers, lower for retail
- **Collection-based limits** — Apply limits across an entire product collection
