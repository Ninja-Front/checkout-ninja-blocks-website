## Checkout Rules

Set the rules. Your checkout enforces them automatically. Prevent risky or invalid orders before they cost you money — no manual review needed.

### How It Works

Checkout Rules let you define conditions and actions. When a condition is met, the checkout can display a warning message, show an error, or block the order entirely.

### Common Use Cases

- **Block PO Box addresses** — Prevent orders to addresses you can't ship to
- **Require minimum order values** — Ensure every order meets your minimum threshold
- **Block specific countries** — Restrict checkout for regions you don't serve
- **Prevent duplicate orders** — Stop customers from accidentally placing the same order twice
- **Enforce product combinations** — Require certain products to be purchased together
- **Holiday cutoffs** — Block orders that can't be delivered by a specific date
- **Fraud prevention** — Flag or block orders matching suspicious patterns
