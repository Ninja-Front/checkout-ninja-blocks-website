## Gift Card Validation

Validate gift cards against your external systems in real-time — right at checkout. Check balances, verify codes, and prevent fraud before the order goes through.

### How It Works

Connect Checkout Ninja to your gift card provider's API. When a customer enters a gift card code, it's validated in real-time. The checkout shows the balance, applies the discount, and blocks invalid or fraudged cards.

### Key Features

- **Real-time balance check** — Show the customer their gift card balance before they complete the order
- **External system integration** — Connect to any gift card provider via API
- **Fraud prevention** — Block stolen, expired, or already-redeemed gift cards
- **Partial redemption** — Support partial gift card usage with remaining balance displayed
- **Multiple gift cards** — Allow customers to combine multiple gift cards on a single order
