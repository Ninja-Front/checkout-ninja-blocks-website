## Conditional Visibility

Your checkout adapts automatically. Show or hide any block based on what's in the cart, who's buying, or where they're shipping. With 31 built-in condition types, every customer sees a checkout built just for them.

### Condition Categories

#### Cart Conditions
- Cart total (above/below threshold)
- Cart item count
- Specific product in cart
- Specific collection in cart
- Cart weight
- Discount code applied

#### Customer Conditions
- New vs returning customer
- Customer tag
- Customer email domain
- Customer order count
- B2B vs D2C customer

#### Shipping & Location
- Shipping country
- Shipping state/province
- Shipping method selected
- Delivery zone

#### Product Conditions
- Product type
- Product vendor
- Product tag
- SKU match
- Variant-level conditions

#### Time & Date
- Day of week
- Time of day
- Date range (for promotions)

#### Other
- Language/locale
- Currency
- Market (Shopify Markets)
- Device type (mobile/desktop)
- Custom metafield values
